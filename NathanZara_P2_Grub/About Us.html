Grub.

Grub is a company that provides for people who take an interest in tasting, and creating meals. Access to endless recipies, Grub enables an interactive space for users to be able to share, use, and give feedback on recipies that may be of ones own creation, or from an inspirational source.

Not every recipe turns out to be what you desire, with Grub you are able to download a tasting sample for each recipe located on our site to ensure it is as good as you hope. The tasting sample is downloaded as a PDF file that you then print through your 3D food printer located in your household.
